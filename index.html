Determination of Phytochemical, Antimicrobial and Antioxidant Potential of Ethanolic Leaf Extract of Rosmarinus officinalis Against Staphylococcus aureus and Escherichia coli

Study Area: Ndagani, Tharaka Nithi County, Kenya

Abstract

This study seeks to determine the phytochemical composition, antimicrobial activity, and antioxidant potential of ethanolic leaf extracts of Rosmarinus officinalis. The extract is evaluated against Staphylococcus aureus and Escherichia coli, which are clinically significant pathogenic bacteria. The research aims to support the use of medicinal plants as alternative therapeutic agents.
