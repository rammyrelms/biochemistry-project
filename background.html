Background and Literature Review

Medicinal plants have long been used in traditional medicine for the management of infectious diseases. Rosmarinus officinalis belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is known for its antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties.

The emergence of antibiotic-resistant bacteria such as Staphylococcus aureus and Escherichia coli has increased the need for alternative antimicrobial agents derived from natural sources.
