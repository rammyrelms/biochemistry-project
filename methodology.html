Methodology

Sample Collection

Fresh leaves of Rosmarinus officinalis will be collected from Ndagani, Tharaka Nithi County, Kenya, identified, washed, and air-dried.

Extraction Procedure

The dried leaves will be ground into powder and extracted using ethanol. The extract will be filtered and concentrated for analysis.

Phytochemical Screening

Qualitative tests will be conducted to detect alkaloids, flavonoids, tannins, saponins, and phenolic compounds.

Antimicrobial Assay

Antimicrobial activity will be assessed against Staphylococcus aureus and Escherichia coli using standard laboratory techniques such as the agar diffusion method.
